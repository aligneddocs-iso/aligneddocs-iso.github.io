Before you order

What IT services company owners ask before ordering.

Does an IT services company really need ISO 9001?
If you sell to enterprises, the public sector, or regulated industries, ISO 9001 is increasingly a procurement gate. Many RFPs and vendor onboarding checklists list it as mandatory — often together with ISO 27001. Without it, capable IT firms are filtered out before their proposal is even read. The quality manual is the documentation foundation that lets you enter that process.
How does ISO 9001 relate to ISO 27001 for IT companies?
They are separate standards: ISO 9001 covers quality management (consistent, repeatable delivery), ISO 27001 covers information security. Many IT providers pursue both. AlignedDocs delivers your ISO 9001 quality manual — the management-system foundation. It does not cover ISO 27001 controls, but a clean ISO 9001 base makes adding 27001 later more straightforward.
Is this suitable for a small MSP or software house under 50 staff?
Yes, it is built specifically for companies with up to 50 employees. The manual is generated from your real business information — your service lines, your delivery processes, your team. No enterprise bloat, just a lean, standard-compliant foundation that reflects how your IT business actually operates.
Can I take this manual into a Stage 1 audit?
Yes — it is structured as a Stage 1 foundation manual: organisational context, quality policy, process descriptions, roles, and risk overview, formatted to ISO 9001:2015 and delivered print-ready. It does not replace a certified auditor or guarantee certification, but it is the document an auditor expects to see before the formal audit.
