How AlignedDocs works

A clear and structured process – no consulting, no certification promises.

The process

1. Data collection

You answer structured questions about your company, processes and scope.

2. Document generation

Based on your input, an individual, ISO-aligned handbook is created.

3. PDF delivery

You receive a professionally structured PDF as the foundation of your quality management system.

What you receive

  • Structured ISO 9001 handbook (PDF)
  • Clear chapters and process descriptions
  • Designed for small businesses (1–20 employees)
  • Editable and extendable internally

Important notice

AlignedDocs does not replace ISO consultants, auditors or certification bodies. It provides documentation only, not certification.
