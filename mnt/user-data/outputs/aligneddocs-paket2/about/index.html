About AlignedDocs

One person.
One clear promise.

AlignedDocs is run by Daniel Fernandes from Böblingen, Germany. No team in the stock-photo sense — just careful work for small businesses that need real documentation.

Why this exists

A friend paid four thousand euros for an ISO 9001 manual that neither he nor his team could understand or maintain. That is the gap AlignedDocs closes.

Small businesses deserve documentation written for how they actually work — not a corporate boilerplate that sits on a shelf.

What I promise

An honest scope. AlignedDocs prepares a Stage 1 foundation manual — not a certification, not a consultancy engagement, not a replacement for an auditor.

An honest delivery time. Usually 2–4 working days. If something delays your order, I tell you before the deadline, not after.

An honest refund. If the manual fundamentally does not reflect your business, write back and we find a solution. Quietly, without arguing.

What this is not

Not a 500-customer marketplace. Not a Trustpilot stack. Not stock photos pretending to be a team. Not a sales funnel disguised as a website.

Just one founder, one product, one inbox.
