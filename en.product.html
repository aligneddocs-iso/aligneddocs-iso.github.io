What is AlignedDocs?

AlignedDocs is a documentation tool that generates a structured ISO-9001 handbook based on your company input.

How it works

1. Data collection

You provide company data, processes and responsibilities through a guided form.

2. Structuring

Your input is transformed into an ISO-9001-aligned structure with logical consistency.

3. PDF handbook

You receive an individual ISO-9001 handbook as a PDF for internal use and further development.

What is included?

  • Cover page & table of contents
  • QMS scope definition
  • Process overview & responsibilities
  • Document control structure

All content is generated strictly from your input and serves as a structured QMS foundation.
