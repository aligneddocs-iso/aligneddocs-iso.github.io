AlignedDocs is an independent one-person practice based in Germany. No team in the stock-photo sense — just careful, direct work for small businesses that need real documentation they can actually own and maintain.

01Why this exists
“

A friend paid four thousand euros for an ISO 9001 manual that neither he nor his team could understand or maintain. The moment the consultant left, the document froze — every small change meant another invoice, every internal audit another booking. He owned a binder, not a system.

Here is what too few people say out loud: a small business can build and run its ISO 9001 documentation itself. The standard does not require a consultant. It asks you to describe how you already work, keep a few records, and review them honestly once a year. That is a foundation you can maintain in-house — without recurring advisory fees.

Small businesses deserve documentation written for how they actually work — not corporate boilerplate that sits on a shelf. AlignedDocs gives you that starting point as a clean, editable manual you keep. The internal audits, the yearly review, the small adjustments: all things a small team can handle on its own, once the structure is there.

02What I promise

An honest scope. AlignedDocs prepares a Stage 1 foundation manual — not a certification, not a consultancy engagement, not a replacement for an auditor.

An honest delivery time. Up to 3 working days. If something delays your order, you hear about it before the deadline, not after.

An honest refund. If the manual fundamentally does not reflect your business, write back and we find a solution. Quietly, without arguing.
