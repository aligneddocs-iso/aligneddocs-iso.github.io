Privacy Policy

Your data is treated with confidentiality and used solely to deliver the service you requested. This policy explains what we collect, why, on what legal basis, and your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Controller

Responsible for the processing of personal data within the meaning of Art. 4(7) GDPR is:

Daniel Fernandes · AlignedDocs
Contact: team@aligneddocs.com

2. Data we collect

We collect only the information you actively provide through our order form: company details (name, industry, size, location), quality policy and objectives, process descriptions, contact person, and the email address to which we deliver the manual. We do not use tracking cookies, do not maintain user accounts, and do not run third-party analytics on this site.

3. Purpose & legal basis

We process your data exclusively to fulfil your order and deliver the requested ISO 9001 quality manual. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — performance of a contract. The email address is also used to contact you about your order on the same legal basis.

4. Service providers & third countries

Your form data is processed by service providers acting on our instructions (Art. 28 GDPR processors). This includes our workflow infrastructure and an AI language model provider that drafts your manual from your inputs. The AI provider may be located outside the EEA, including the United States. Transfers are protected by the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework and EU Standard Contractual Clauses. We do not authorise these providers to use your data for model training.

5. Storage & retention

Your form data is retained as long as needed to fulfil your order and meet legal obligations (typically up to 10 years for invoicing data under §257 HGB). Free-text business descriptions are deleted after confirmed delivery.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access (Art. 15), correct (Art. 16), delete (Art. 17), restrict (Art. 18), object to (Art. 21), and port (Art. 20) your data. Contact: team@aligneddocs.com. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard technical and organisational measures including TLS encryption in transit and access controls on stored data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect service changes or legal requirements. Last updated: May 2026.

This is a self-drafted policy. For productive use, have a qualified data protection lawyer review and adapt it before go-live.
